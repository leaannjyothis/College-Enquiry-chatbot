COURSES

B.TECH Computer Science and Engineering

Established in 2003, the Computer Science & Engineering Department at VJCET offers a B.Tech. program with an intake of 180 students and is affiliated with A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technological University. Supported by highly qualified faculty with extensive teaching and industry experience, the department is known for strong research output and global alumni connections. Students benefit from internships at top MNCs and achieve placements in esteemed organizations, with a focus on holistic development to equip them for long-term success.





B.TECH Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering Department at VJCET, established in 2006, offers B.Tech programs with robust infrastructure, experienced faculty, and well-equipped labs. The department fosters technical and extracurricular growth through its Civil Engineering association and bridges the industry-academia gap with active student participation in consultancy projects and government-funded initiatives. With strong placement opportunities and a commitment to producing world-class engineers, the department plays a vital role in shaping the future of its students.





B.TECH Mechanical Engineering

Since its inception in 2003, the Mechanical Engineering Department at VJCET, has evolved into the largest department, offering quality education through comprehensive practical and theoretical training. With an intake of 90 students across two batches and 18 dedicated faculty members (six with doctorates), the department supports robust academic and practical experiences through eight advanced laboratories and workshops. Students excel in innovative projects, winning awards and completing prestigious ISRO and SERB projects, while benefiting from industry exposure, specialized courses, and active participation in the annual 'Techfest' to enhance their readiness for professional challenges.





B.TECH Electronics & Communication Engineering

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) offers a B.Tech program with an intake of 60 students. Equipped with modern classrooms and laboratories, the department promotes practical learning and holistic student development. With a committed and experienced faculty, the department aspires to be recognized for excellence and innovation in education, research, and professional service both nationally and internationally.





B.TECH Electrical & Electronics Engineering

The Department offers B.Tech Program in Electrical & Electronics Engineering. The academic offering is focused on the theoretical and practical aspects of Electrical Machines, Control Systems, Power Systems, Electrical and Electronic devices & circuits, Instrumentation etc. The programme prepares students for prospective careers in Energetics, Power Generation, Transmission and Distribution and in traditional industries requiring powered machinery, power electronics, automated instrumentation controls, etc.





BTECH Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AI) offers a 4-year B.Tech program with an intake of 60 students, focusing on core computer science and specialized AI courses under KTU. With applications in diverse fields like healthcare, robotics, and finance, the program equips students for roles such as Data Scientist. Supported by dedicated faculty, the department aims to excel in education and research, preparing students with strong technical, interpersonal, and problem-solving skills.





B.Tech in Computer Science and Design

B.Tech in Computer Science and Design Engineering is a four-year postgraduate program that focuses on the design, analysis, and construction of resilient structures. It covers advanced topics like earthquake engineering, bridge engineering, and structural dynamics, equipping students with modern tools for sustainable and efficient design. Graduates gain skills essential for careers in construction, infrastructure development, and consulting.





MBA

The Department offers a two-year Master program affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, providing comprehensive coverage of the prescribed curriculum. To enhance students' competitive edge for placements and workplace challenges, the Department organizes specialized academic activities. Additionally, MBA students have access to dedicated one-to-one computing facilities in a well-equipped lab with internet connectivity during office hours.
